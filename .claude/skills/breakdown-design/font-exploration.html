Designed by IBM as a corporate typography system — its DNA is engineered, neutral, legible. Plex Mono is a true companion (not a fallback), and the slashed zero, clean 1/l/I, and tabular figures make it perfect for the JSON list and money amounts. The whole system reads "built by engineers" without trying too hard.

+ Strongest fit for the brand promise.   + Free, OFL.   - Slightly seen-around — used by IBM products and many privacy-leaning tools.
